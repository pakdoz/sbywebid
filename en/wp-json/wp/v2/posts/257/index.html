The history of the battle to defend independence in Surabaya cannot be separated from the role of "Nahdlatul Ulama (NU) Jihad Resolution" on October 22, 1945. The Jihad Resolution, which was closely related to the November 10 battle, was adopted in an old building not far from Hero Monument. The building is located north of the Heroes Monument, precisely in the village of Bubutan VI\/2 Surabaya. The building is now NU Branch Office Secretariat (PCNU) Surabaya. On October 23, 2011 the place was made “Jihad Resolution Monument” inaugurated by the general chairman of PBNU, Prof. KH.
