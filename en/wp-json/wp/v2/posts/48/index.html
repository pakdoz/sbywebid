\n

Tugu Pahlawan is a monument built to commemorate the services of heroes who fought to defend Indonesia's independence in Surabaya.\n

\n

\n

Before it was built, during the colonial period in the area there were buildings Raad Van Justitie or the court building belonging to the Dutch East Indies Government in Surabaya.\n
